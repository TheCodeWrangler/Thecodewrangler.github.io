About Prairie Tellers

We are dedicated to preserving and celebrating the art of storytelling in the American Midwest.

Founded in 2005, Prairie Tellers brings together storytellers from across the region to share personal narratives, folktales, and historical accounts that connect us to our shared heritage. Our community-based organization hosts regular events, workshops, and an annual festival that attracts visitors from around the country.

Through the power of oral tradition, we aim to strengthen community bonds, preserve local history, and inspire the next generation of storytellers.
